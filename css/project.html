    Software Engineer

    A software engineer is a professional who designs, develops, tests, and maintains software applications and systems. They apply engineering principles to software creation, ensuring that the programs they build are efficient, reliable, and scalable. Software engineers work with various programming languages, frameworks, and tools to create everything from mobile apps to large-scale enterprise systems. Their role often involves problem-solving, debugging, collaborating with teams, and ensuring that software meets both technical and user requirements. They are essential in industries ranging from tech to finance, healthcare, and beyond.
